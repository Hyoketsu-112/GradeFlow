How It Works

From Excel to Report Card in 3 Steps

No technical knowledge required. If you can use a phone, you can use GradeFlow.

📤
1
Upload Your Score Sheet

Drag and drop your Excel or CSV file. GradeFlow automatically reads student names, test scores, practical scores, and exam scores. No reformatting needed.

⚡
2
Grades Compute Instantly

GradeFlow calculates totals, assigns grades (A–F), generates remarks, and ranks students by position — all in under 3 seconds. You can edit any score manually too.

📄
3
Download Beautiful PDFs

Export a professional A4 report card for each student — with your school name, teacher name, subject breakdown, grade, and remark. Ready to print or share digitally.

Features

Everything a Teacher Needs

Built by studying how Nigerian teachers actually work — not how we think they work.

📊
Excel & CSV Upload

Upload any score sheet format. GradeFlow intelligently detects columns for names, test, practical, and exam scores — even from messy files.

🧮
Auto Grade Computation

Scores are computed the moment you upload. Test (20) + Practical (20) + Exam (60) = Total (100). No calculator needed. No errors possible.

📄
Branded PDF Report Cards

Each student gets a clean, professional A4 report card carrying your school name, class, teacher, scores, grade, remark, and class position.

✏️
Inline Score Editing

Click any score to edit it directly in the table. Grades and totals update in real time. Perfect for last-minute corrections before exporting.

🏆
Class Position Ranking

Students are automatically ranked 1st, 2nd, 3rd... based on total score. Tied students share the same position. Fully automatic.

📶
Works Offline

No internet? No problem. GradeFlow saves all your data locally. Use it during exams, in remote schools, or anywhere — online or not.

⚠️
Smart Validation

Missing scores are flagged instantly. Scores above the maximum are highlighted. No grade is assigned until an exam score exists. Zero errors.

🏫
Multi-Class Management

Manage JSS1A, JSS2B, SS3C — all from one dashboard. Switch between classes instantly. Each class keeps its own independent records.

📈
Performance Analytics

See class averages, highest and lowest scores, pass rates, and grade distributions at a glance. Identify struggling students before it's too late.
